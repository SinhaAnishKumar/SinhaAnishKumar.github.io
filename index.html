About Me

This is it. ;)

Anish Kr. Sinha is an Indian UI/UX Designer, Illustrator, Front-End Developer by passion having expertise and years of experience working on these technologies. While delivering quality work with a passion in creating meaningful and fuctional user experiences for iOS, Android and the Web...Alongside, he can work as Graphic Designer too. Typically, he’s Driven & Permanently Curious and obsessed with designing things and even more obsessed with designing cool & clean stuffs. He has been in the business of designing and creating since he hung his first painting on the wall when he was 11.

His interests, however, extend beyond the Web and he even loves making 3D Models and 2D/3D Floor Plans. He holds a Bachelor Degree in Computer Applications. During his graduation, he has been actively involved in the design community for the 3 years. He has designed user experiences for small businesses, schools, events, nonprofits and more. Basically, He is a developer with Good Design-Sense.

Currently he’s based in Delhi, India. Where he’s working as an independent creative and Application Development Analyst at Accenture Solutions Pvt. Ltd. since Dec 2022

When he's not designing, he’s probably hanging out with his girlfriend, watching series, sketching or messing around something on YouTube.
